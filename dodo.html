Dodo

Swipe cards to curate your present moment

Powerful GTD apps like Omnifocus are great for professional project management. But for tasks in everyday life, a giant GTD app may take more time to write down a laundry routine than actually doing it.

To find a to-do app for daily use, I tried more than 30 apps that target to simplify the to-do list experience. Some are fancy, some are delightful, but zero that I kept using. The most handy, however, is still a pack of sticky note and a sharpie.

That being said, two apps amazed me by how they simplify the to-do experience, yet maintain high effectiveness: Blink and Later.
